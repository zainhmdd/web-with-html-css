Return & Exchange Policy

Not Satisfied with Your Order?

You can exchange it for a different size or product, or return it for a full refund. It's simple and stress-free!

* Sale items are not eligible for return or exchange.

Easy Steps

1. Check Your Item

Ensure items are unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

2. Submit a Request

Fill out the form in "My Orders" or the return/exchange form included in your package.

3. Ship Your Item

Use the return label provided to send the item back to us securely.

4. Confirmation

We'll notify you via email once your refund or exchange is processed.

Return & Exchange Conditions

  • Items must be returned within 30 days.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with tags attached.
  • Sale items and personal items are non-returnable.
