Size Guide

Get the perfect fit by referring to our size guide. If you're between sizes, we recommend choosing the larger size for a more comfortable fit.

How to Measure

Follow these simple steps to measure yourself:

  • Waist: Measure around the smallest part of the waist.
  • Hips: Measure around about 20cm below the waist. Be steady and don't move the measuring tape up and down!

    If you're not too sure of what size you are, follow the steps above and pay close attention to the Size Info available on our every item!
