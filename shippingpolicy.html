SHIPPING POLICY
YES, IT'S TRUE. WE SHIP EVERYWHERE!
You read that right. We can ship to wherever you may be in the world. Don't forget to read our detailed Domestic & International shipping policy below for more information!
DOMESTIC DELIVERIES
We use JNE Shipping Company (http://www.jne.co.id) for shipping to all cities in Indonesia. JNE offers Economical, Regular and Fast Service. Below you can find the delivery schedule.
INTERNATIONAL DELIVERIES We ship worldwide! We use EMS Pos Indonesia (http://ems.posindonesia.co.id/) for all overseas shipping.
For Singapore addresses, we use MBC Express Shipping which offers more affordable shipping fee.
For international deliveries, EMS should take 8-14 days depending on your delivery area.
ORDER PROCESSING TIME We only process your order during business days, from Monday to Friday; but we ship from Monday to Saturday (orders confirmed after 12 pm will be processed on the next working day).

All confirmed orders (through 'CONFIRM PAYMENT' menu) will be processed on the following day.

It takes 3-4 working days to process your order before we send it to shipping company.

We will ship your order if there are no problems occurring in terms of payment.

If you confirm on (starting from 00.01 am), your order will be processed on Monday of the next week. Note that our order process contains of checking the payment, preparing and delivering the goods to the shipping company.

We will process and ship based on your order ID. If you have more than one order ID, unfortunately, no combined-orders possible for shipment.
RATES Domestic rates can be found at http://www.jne.co.id and international rates informed upon request.
SHIPPING RULES & RESTRICTIONS COTTONINK is not responsible for any risks of loss of the merchandise after JNE has delivered the packages to the designated address

Customers are responsible if JNE can't deliver the goods on time due to incomplete address, no recipient at the address or the recipient rejects the goods.

If the goods are returned to us, customers are obligated to pay the shipping fee one more time if the mistakes are due to customer's conditions.

If there are any shipping errors coming from COTTONINK, we guarantee that we will give free shipping to the customers.

We also are not responsible for any additional taxes and duties charged. If you decide to refuse the delivery, you are responsible in all fees charged by shipping company - both for sending and returning the product to us.
